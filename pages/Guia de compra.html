HOW TO BUY

To make a purchase in our e-shop, here's what you need to do. For example:

  1. Choose the product you want to buy.
  2. Click on the "Add to Cart" button. This will add the product to your cart and take you to it.
  3. You can continue adding other products to your cart, or click on "Checkout" to proceed.
  4. Fill in your contact information and click on "Continue".
  5. Enter the shipping address where you want to receive the product. Then click on "Continue".
  6. Select the desired shipping method and click on "Continue".
  7. Choose your payment method.
  8. You can also select the option of "Bank Transfer".
  9. Once you have chosen the payment method, click on "Continue".
  10. Finally, on the Order Confirmation page, you can review all the purchase information. Then click on "Continue".
  11. You will be redirected to another screen to complete the details of the chosen payment method. After confirming the purchase, you will receive an email from us. However, this email will not serve as a payment receipt.

Once the payment is confirmed, we will proceed with the shipping of the purchased products. We offer the following payment methods:

  • Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer
